Short Information About Our Team Members

Otar Javakhishvili

Otar is an electrical engineer and programmer. He has an experience in electronics and PCB design. He works mostly in Altium designer and EAGLE. otar has also experience in FPGA and VHDL using vivado, vivado HLS. He has worked a lot on 8 bit micro controllers using embedded C, also he has used Arduino and Raspberry pi. He has experience in controlling mechanical devices with his custom made electronics and firmware/software. He has developed several control systems for particle detector mechanical systems with network interface and custom GUIs written in Python and communication done with TCP client-server system or EPICS control system.

Otar has Bachelor's, Master's and PHD degrees of the Agricultural University of Georgia. He is great schematics designer, analog and digital circuits designer, PCB designer (design, fabrication, testing and troubleshooting). And that's not all. See his publications here .
